About Me

Professor of Management and International Trade with a passion for academic leadership

Dr. MPA ISS

I am a professor of management and international trade with extensive experience spanning economic research, policy analysis, and academic leadership. My work has focused on understanding international trade, management principles, and their impact on institutional growth and development.

Throughout my career, I have held leadership positions at the University of Puerto Rico, including Academic Director, Dean of the School of Business Administration, Director of the University Press, and Chancellor of the UPR Humacao campus. I have also been a guest professor at universities across the United States, Europe, Africa, Latin America, and the Caribbean.

Education & Academic Achievements

  • Doctorate in Administrative Sciences - Complutense University of Madrid, Spain (1991)
  • Master's in Public Administration - University of Puerto Rico, Río Piedras (1987)
  • Diploma in International Human Rights and International Relations - University of Strasbourg, France (1989)
  • Specialized Studies in International Trade and Economics - University of South Carolina, USA (1994) and Memphis, Tennessee, USA (1997)
  • Bachelor of Social Sciences - University of Puerto Rico, Río Piedras (1985)
